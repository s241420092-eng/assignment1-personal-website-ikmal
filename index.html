ROBIN LIM

Robin Lim is a Malaysian entrepreneur and modern farmer best known as the founder of Superfruits Valley, one of Southeast Asia’s largest superfruit farms located in Chuping, Perlis. Before entering agriculture, he worked in the hospitality and construction industries, but his passion for farming and nutritional food security led him to establish the farm in 2016. Starting with the idea of making rare and highly nutritious fruits more accessible in Malaysia, he successfully cultivated crops such as figs, gac fruit, lemons, finger limes, mulberries, and passionfruit using sustainable and pesticide-free farming methods. Today, the estate spans around 25–50 hectares with more than 150,000 plants and has been recognised by the Northern Corridor Implementation Authority (NCIA) as a flagship modern agriculture project.

Modern Farming Technology

Robin Lim is also recognised for pioneering Malaysia’s commercial lemon farming industry and promoting smart agriculture through IoT-based farming systems and sustainable composting practices. Under his leadership, Superfruits Valley supplies premium produce to many leading Malaysian restaurants and hotels, including fine-dining establishments and luxury resorts. During the Covid-19 pandemic, he expanded the business by creating Fruju, a freeze-dried superfruit health drink brand that gained strong market demand across Malaysia. Beyond farming, he collaborates with universities on agricultural research, supports local agro-tourism in Perlis, and advocates for developing the next generation of Malaysian farmers through modern and environmentally responsible agriculture.
